Chicken Alfredo Pasta

Delicious middle of the week recipe

A creamy, luxurious dish that combines tender pieces of chicken with perfectly cooked pasta, all enveloped in a rich Alfredo sauce. Ideal for weeknight dinners or special occasions.

Ingredients

For the Alfredo Sauce

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup Parmesan cheese

For the Pasta and Chicken:

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup Parmesan cheese

Preparation

  1. Cook fettuccine according to package instructions.
  2. Season chicken breasts with salt and pepper, then cook in a skillet until done. Slice into strips.
  3. For the Alfredo sauce, melt butter in a pan, add heavy cream and bring to a simmer. Stir in Parmesan cheese until smooth.
  4. Combine pasta, chicken, and sauce. Serve immediately.
