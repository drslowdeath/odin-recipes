Vegeterian Stir Fry

Just as healthy as it is delicious

A quick and colorful stir-fry packed with a variety of vegetables and tofu. It's a nutritious and satisfying meal that can be whipped up in no time.

Ingredients

For the Stir-Fry:

  • 1 block tofu
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce

Preparation

  1. Cut tofu into cubes and saut√© in a pan until golden.
  2. Add mixed vegetables to the pan and stir-fry until tender.
  3. Add soy sauce and toss to coat.
  4. Serve over rice or noodles.
