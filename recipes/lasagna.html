Lasagna

Just like mama used to make

Indulge in the ultimate comfort food with this classic lasagna recipe that promises layers of rich, meaty goodness. This dish is a harmonious blend of creamy béchamel sauce, robust meat sauce, and perfectly cooked pasta, all brought together by a generous sprinkling of gooey, melted cheese. Ideal for family dinners, potlucks, or any occasion that calls for a hearty meal, this lasagna is not just a dish—it's an experience. Prepare to wow your guests and satisfy even the most discerning palates.

Ingredients

For the Meat Sauce:

For the Meat Sauce:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 lb ground pork or sausage
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3-4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (14 oz) tomato sauce
  • 1 can (6 oz) tomato paste
  • 2 tsp dried oregano
  • 2 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Béchamel Sauce:

  • 4 cups whole milk
  • 4 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg
  • Salt and white pepper to taste

For the Pasta and Cheese Layers:

  • 9-12 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Optional:

  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish
